Future Nostalgia


By the Dua Lipa


Future Nostalgia is the second studio album by British singer-songwriter Dua Lipa, released on March 27, 2020 via Warner Music. The album has a pop and disco sound. The record received favorable reviews and became a commercial success, reaching the top of the charts in 13 countries.
